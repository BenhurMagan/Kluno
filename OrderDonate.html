How to Order

Navigate to down the website and find Lawrence's email and contact him about your order. During your email include the following:

  • What article of clothing do you want? e.g. Shirt or Hoodie
  • What colour is the clothing?
  • What design would you like to be in the clothing? e.g. Design in the website or Custom

Donate your own article of clothing!

Donating your own shirt or hoodie to be designed will come at a lower price when purchased. To design your donated clothes you only need to include the following during your email:

  • Is this item a donation or a request for a redesign?
  • If the donated item is a request for a redesign, then what design would you like? e.g. Design in the website or Custom

Donation

Why should you Donate?

Donating clothes to Kluno not only decreases waste but also help those in need. With every clothes donated more redesigned clothes are produced and sold at an affordable price. Clothes that custommers do not want to redesign or save will be donated to St Vincent De Paul. 10% of the profit made from Kluno will be donated to the Christchurch City Mission.
