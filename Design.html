Design Categories

Cultural

One of the designs you can request is Cultural Designs. This includes any culture including Filipino and Maori culture.

Anime

Another design category you can request are Anime Images. Whilst odering your custom design you can include the image you want to design the shirt with.

Cartoons

The last design category that are the most commonly ordered are Cartoon Images. Similar to Anime Images you can also include your desired design whilst ordering.
