Kluno

Kluno is a company that strives to make a change by receiving unused shirts and redesigning them to be sold at an affordable price. The company's goal is to contribute in recyling wasted clothes that are in good condition. This is because only 15% of clothing are recycled annually.

This is one of the designs illustrated by Lawrence Ventura the creator of the company Kluno. The image is inspired by the massive anime franchise, Pokemon.
