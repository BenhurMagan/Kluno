About Kluno...

Kluno is a business originated from the kura, Catholic Cathedral College. This company is made to save old clothes and make them useful again. The mission for Kluno is to restore the usefulness of old clothes in customers' closets and make them wearable and stylish again. Kluno be using transfer paper to apply designs that the customer would like. With the restoration of old clothes Kluno will also be selling new shirts that customers can buy, Kluno will be adding original designs in the shirts and will also be doing custom jobs for a higher price.

Founder

Lawrence is the creator of Kluno which repurposes old and worn shirts and adds designs to repurpose them to reduce waste. He draws inspiration from other companies that try to alleviate their environmental impact on making their product. Lawrence is a hard working individual who is well versed in the creative fields. He is the captain of the Senior A Volleyball team whilst balancing both school and his job. He is the secretary for the Young Vinnies group helping others in need through random acts of kindness and volunteering to the St. Vincent De Paul shops. He merged his passion for helping others as well as his creative skills to make a change and help out.

Purpose/Vision

The purpose of Kluno is to take part in recycling of used clothing. Only 15% of used clothing are being recycled annually. Therefore by redesigning donated clothing, whether it be in bad or perfect condition, less waste is made. Lawrence also would like to promote his designs and business skills through this website.

Values

Recycling

Recyling is the main value of Kluno. Repurposing uneeded clothing and redesigning it to sell and reduce waste.

Saving

Saving is a byproduct of Kluno. Compared to buying clothes on other stores, Kluno will have products that are affordable despite its professional designs.
